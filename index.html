Expense Tracker

This is the Web Expense Tracker created using HTML, CSS and JS as an university project. You can use this website to keep track of your expenses date-wise. Just enter your expenses with the amount and date and the "grand total" figure will reflect the total expenses. You can also delete any expense under the action column of the table.
